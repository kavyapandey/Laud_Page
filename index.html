Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is especially recognized as the Bidrohi Kobi(rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philosipher of Bangladesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writings deal with the revolution of that period of the British Government. His revolutionalry attempt helped him to be the national poet of Bangladesh.He has raised his voice against any facism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awesome Writer

Nazrul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity and revolution. He opposed all kinds of bigotry and fundamentalism, including religious, caste-based and gender-based. Throughout his career, Nazrul wrote short stories, novels and essays but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new music form such as Bengali ghazals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and gramphone records), collectively known as Nazrul geet.

"Love has no meaning or amount."-Kazi Nazrul Islam
